# Visual direction

## Shared language

The series is built as cinematic documentary photography: soft natural light,
Kodak Portra color, humanistic observation, A24 atmospheric realism, shallow
depth of field, subtle emotion, and one clear symbol per frame. Images contain
no quotes, logos, stock smiles, sensational violence, or motivational staging.

## Reusable image prompt

```text
Create a portrait 4:5 social media image.
Use case: photorealistic-natural.
Asset type: editorial image for a personal blog post.
Theme: <post theme>.
Main emotion: <emotion>.
Main symbol: <one human-scale symbol>.
Scene: <one restrained documentary moment>.
Style: cinematic documentary photography, humanistic photography,
soft natural light, Kodak Portra film texture, National Geographic
storytelling, A24 atmospheric realism, shallow depth of field,
subtle emotions, visual poetry.
Constraints: minimal objects, real-life atmosphere, emotional honesty.
Avoid: text, typography, logos, watermark, stock smiles, sensationalism,
motivational staging, excessive prison-bar imagery.
```
