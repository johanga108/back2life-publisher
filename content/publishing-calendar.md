# Back2Life publishing calendar

The calendar contains two interleaved four-day sequences: visual detention
experience posts starting on Monday, June 8, 2026, and short practice/life
posts starting on Wednesday, June 10, 2026. Together they usually create
one publication every two days, with a few one-day light shifts to avoid
known scheduled-content conflicts.
All publications are scheduled for 19:00 Europe/Moscow.

Assumptions:

- Sundays and month-end dates are allowed for this merged plan.
- The publisher should run at 19:00 Europe/Moscow; earlier ordinary runs exit
  without publishing.
- Some June/July posts are shifted by one day to avoid already scheduled content.
- Practice/life posts still need generated images before Instagram
  publication; until an image path is attached, they are marked as
  `image pending`.
- Dates, titles, and texts are editable in the Google Sheets tab `Codex`;
  the publisher reads that tab before each run.
